Topology optimization

Topology optimization is a mathematical method used in engineering design to determine the optimal material layout within a given design space, for a given set of loads, boundary conditions, and constraints, with the goal of maximizing performance and efficiency. This process involves redistributing material within the design space to achieve the best structural performance, often resulting in innovative and efficient designs that may not be intuitive or easily conceived by traditional design methods.
